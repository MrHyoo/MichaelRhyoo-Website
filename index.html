Michael Rhyoo

Cook

I am from the Los Angeles region of California, United States of America. I have been living in California for my entire life and I am eager to continue in my journey in culinary. I have been in culinary since I was very young, in fact I was 10 years old. This journey begins with me in the kitchen with my mother helping her cook dinner. This experience opened my eyes to the world of culinary. While ironically my mother does not like to cook, I developed a love for the craft and my passion has only grown throughout the years.

Cooking is very important to me because I have been doing to for many years and to me it is a gateway to gathering people together to spend time in happiness. Good food boosts one's mood leading them to becoming more happy and spreading that happiness to others around them. This ability to bring others joy is what drives me to continue to excel in my skills as a cook and leads me to create new dishes that would both surprise and please the palate of my guests.

I know how to cook in many different cultures and have experience in the real world. When cooking one must be aware and prepared for many different senarios and willing to do work that others will not want to do. This is necessary to further your career and become a more well rounded part of the kitchen benefiting your team and your customers. Some skills that I benefit by having are time management, knife technique, mental and physical agility. These allow me to fluidly move through the kitchen, operating at a high speed while still being able to maintain a level of percision and prestige.
